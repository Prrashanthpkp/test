About Me

I’m P.K. Prrashanth, a passionate Front-end Developer and Game Designer. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, I create responsive websites that are both functional and visually appealing. Alongside web development, I specialize in crafting immersive gaming experiences using Unreal Engine and Blender.

Over the years, I’ve worked on a wide range of projects — from VR walkthroughs to fast-paced Unreal Engine action games. My goal is to bridge creativity and technology, designing interactive experiences that leave a lasting impact.

I pursued my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in Gaming Technology at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Ramapuram. This gave me a strong academic foundation in both software development and game design. Currently, I’m further enhancing my skills by pursuing a Full-Stack Development (Java) course at IT Vedant.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, collaborating with teams, and continuously improving my skills to stay ahead in the fast-evolving world of development and design.
